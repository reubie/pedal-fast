    About PEDAL-FAST

    A CYCLE A DAY KEEPS THE DOCTOR AWAY

    Pedal fast is a term we coined in one sitting. My team and I have been long interested in competitive sports. Riding bicycles is something most us loved in our childhood, however, it quickly became a thing of the past in our teen years as majority went to boarding high schools. After high school we transitioned to university where resources are limited. Living in the hostels is a blocker when it comes to owning bulky items. Bike sharing has a common phenomenon in major cities around so we thought why not Nairobi. So we came with the idea of bike sharing. Our bike kiosks are strategically located at different points of town. We want everyone to relive their childhood beloved sport. We offer quality bicycles in all sizes and model at flexible charges for flexible hours. In our kiosk are friendly employees ready to assist you in anyway bicycles related that is lol:). Visit us at our various outlets and experience the joys of cycling. Come see the city under the sun on wheels as the wind tassels your hair. Bike sharing is also an effective way of conserving the environment. In this era of pollution what better way to navigate short distances than on the pedals. In these times of obsesity and other health complications bike sharing is also a good form of exercise. We are providing all thes at a minimal cost.
